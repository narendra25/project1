TCS Limited, initially started as "Tata Computer Systems" was founded 1968 by division of Tata Sons Limited.[18] Its early contracts included punched card services to sister company TISCO (now Tata Steel), working on an Inter-Branch Reconciliation System for the Central Bank of India, and providing bureau services to Unit Trust of India.
